ABOUT Emit

Mission Statement

“To provide high quality design, create, manufacture and install the finest of the Electrical systems, Manufacture and Construct other premium products in the industry and fulfill the requirements of the Customers to the best of Satisfaction always”. The functions are:

ELECTRICAL

EMIT offers comprehensive portfolio of end-to-end electrical solutions and support services.

CIVIL

Design and Construction of infrastructure facilities, Industrial Buildings, Switchyards and many other construction related projects.

MECHANICAL

Design, Fabrication, Assembly, Erection of specialized mechanical equipments used in several types of Projects with testing and commissioning.

Why EMIT

EMIT promises to provide top quality Design and Engineering, sales and service of the products which shall meet or exceed the expectations of their customers and well above industry standards.

As a result of our maintaining integrity, approach, transparency and result oriented attitude we have not only gained the reputation but also received many repeat orders and new orders through the references of our wide esteemed customers.

Who we are

Established in 1994, EMIT is an ISO Certified professionally managed, Engineering and Contracting Private Limited Company incorporated in INDIA. The company's main office and manufacturing facility is located in Bangalore, Karnataka State, INDIA. EMIT is one of the leading established company in the EPC (Engineering, Procurement and Construction) contracts sector which involves Construction, Electrical and Mechanical installations, Design, Engineering and manufacturing of Various types of EHV Switchyards, Transmission lines, Distribution Panels etc.

Our products are being widely used in industries like Engineering, Plywood, veneer and bi products, MDF plants, Pharmaceutical, Food Processing, Textiles, Gasses, Breweries, Braveries and Distilleries, weaving and spinning, Construction material, granite processing, chemical, paper & packaging and many more Industries and establishments. Other than this we design and construct Tonnage LPG and CNG plants. We design systems that are Safe, easy to operate, cost effective and known for their superb quality and durability.
